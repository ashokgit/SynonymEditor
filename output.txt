Once upon a time, there was a little girl who lived in a hamlet near the woodland. Whenever she went out, the little girl wore a red riding cloak, so everyone in the village called her Little Red Riding __HOOD__.

One morning, Little Red Riding Hood asked her mother if she could go to visit her grandmother as it had been awhile since they'd encountered each other.

"That's a good suggestion," her mother said. So they packed a nice container for Little Red Riding Hood to take to her grandmother.

When the container was ready, the little girl put on her scarlet cloak and kissed her mother goodbye.

"Remember, go directly to Grandma's house," her mother cautioned. "Don't linger along the way and please don't talk to strangers! The woods are perilous."

"Don't worry, mommy," said Little Red Riding Hood, "I'll be prudent."

Little Red Riding Hood was enjoying the warm summer day so much, that she didn't notice a murky shadow approaching out of the forest behind her...
